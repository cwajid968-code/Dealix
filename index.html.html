The People Behind Dealix

Built on Honest Advice,
Not Just Commissions

SC
Sajid Chohan
Founder, Dealix Property
  • Coverage across every CDA sector in Islamabad
  • Verified listings — no ghost properties
  • Buy, sell, rent, and invest — all under one roof
  • Personalized support from first call to final transfer
  • Transparent fees — we tell you what we earn

I started Dealix Property two years ago because I saw how many people — especially first-time buyers and overseas Pakistanis — were getting misled by agents who cared more about closing quickly than closing correctly. My elder brother had a difficult experience buying a plot in Islamabad a few years back, losing months and money to undisclosed disputes. That stayed with me.

Since opening our office in F-10 Markaz, we have worked with families looking for their first home, investors building long-term portfolios, and expats sending money from abroad who need someone on the ground they can genuinely trust. We cover all CDA sectors — from established areas like F and G series to newer developments — and we do the verification work before we ever present a property to a client.

The goal has always been simple: give the same advice we would give to our own family. If a deal does not make sense for you, we will say so. That honesty is why most of our business comes from referrals, and why we plan to stay in this market for a very long time.
